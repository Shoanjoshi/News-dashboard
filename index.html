This topic cluster encompasses various environmental and social challenges, including the plight of endangered species like the North Atlantic right whale, the impact of fossil fuel dependency on climate change, and the social implications of personal health issues like 'brain fog'. It highlights the intersection of ecological concerns and human experiences in contemporary society.

Subthemes:
  • Endangered Species Conservation
  • Fossil Fuel Phaseout
  • Health and Well-being

Subthemes:
